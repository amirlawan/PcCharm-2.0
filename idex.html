Sustainable Community Development Alliance

Organizational Structure and Implementation Framework

Drafted on: 25 December, 2024

By: Executive Members of SCDA

Vision Statement

“To develop a world in which every community grows sustainably, getting equity, durability, and opportunity for both present and future generations.”

Mission Statement

"To empower communities through innovative and inclusive programs that address environmental, social, and economic challenges, fostering sustainable development in alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals."

Objectives

  • To encourage quality education by providing accessible and inclusive learning opportunities.
  • To eradicate poverty and hunger by implementing sustainable livelihood activities.
  • To improve health and well-being by increasing access to healthcare and tackling major public health concerns.
  • To establish more resilient societies, promote gender equality, renewable energy uptake, and climate action.

Organizational Name

Proposed Names:

  1. Pioneer for Progress
  2. Heritage and Innovation
  3. Community Development Forum
  4. Pioneer for Community Development
  5. Bridge and Hope Initiatives
  6. Youth Awareness and Sustainable Development

Chosen Name: Sustainable Community Development Alliance (SCDA)

Target Audience

  • Local communities
  • Students
  • Children in vulnerable situations
  • Youth groups and other changemakers
  • People with special needs
  • Policymakers and stakeholders in development
